Here are some random movie opinions (hot takes or not)

  • The Nightmare on Elm Street reboot was not bad; I think it is good as a movie and fine as a reboot/remake
  • The Friday the 13th remake was not bad either; I think that the kills were really fun and interesting
  • Practical effects are always better than digital effects
  • Malignant sucked
  • All paranormal horror is garbage. Yes, that means The Conjuring
  • The Forever Purge was awful. It was 2 hours of cowboys shooting at each other, with a really predictable plot
  • You're Next is an amazing slasher film, having some of my favorite slasher killers
  • The Friday the 13th and Halloween franchises aren't that great, but I still respect them
  • Slumber Party Massacre was an incredibly cheesy yet fun slasher that deserves more recognition
  • Hellraiser was not that great, but Clive Barker and Doug Bradley are amazing. I expected more sceentime for the cenobites and only received 5 minutes of it
